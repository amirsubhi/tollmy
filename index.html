tollmy

Open, machine-readable dataset of Malaysian highway toll fares — 31 highways, 224 plazas, 8,591 fare pairs.

Fares are sourced from each operator's published schedule and may be incomplete or outdated. Always verify against the operator's own calculator before relying on any fare for payment.

Dataset files (JSON)

  • concessionaires.json — 16 operators and their highways
  • plazas.json — 224 plazas with coordinates
  • promotions.json — festive/government discounts
